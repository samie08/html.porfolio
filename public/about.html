About Me

Hello! I’m Samuel Obaseki, a Front-End Web Developer from Benin City, Edo State, Nigeria. I have a strong passion for technology and creativity, which led me to pursue a career in web development. Although I graduated from the University of Benin with a degree in International Studies and Diplomacy, I discovered my true calling in tech. I enjoy building user-friendly and visually engaging websites that offer seamless user experiences.

Outside of coding, I enjoy a variety of activities, including playing video games, watching movies, hitting the gym, and playing football.
